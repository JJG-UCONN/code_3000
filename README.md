Personal Ethics Statement:\
I have determined that **transparency** both in what the AI is using as references, as well as when AI is used.  I have determined this as with prolific use of AI becoming more mainstream, I find it important to be able to determine the validity of the material along with the methods it was derived.  Currently such an example of a step in the right direction in terms of transparency on this issue can be seen with Steam's Content Survey for published games containing an AI discourse section which is reviewed by steam before publishing.  This disclosure is then viewable by any user on the storefront page in steam.  Another important value in terms of AI ethics is **independence** from AI use, particularly being able to perform the tasks done by AI without requiring it's help, but rather using it as a tool to supplement what the user can achieve.  Dependence on AI stunts growth of learning and capability of doing it without help; It is therefore necessary that no one depends on AI as to continue encouraging creativity and the pursuit of knowledge.
